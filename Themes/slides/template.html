Heuristic Evaluation

Strengths & trade-offs of Heuristic Evaluation

Strengths
  • Helps designers surface usability issues early in the design process.
  • Fast and low-budget compared to other forms of testing.
  • No need to recruit real customers or participants.
Trade-offs
  • Heavily dependent on the expertise of the evaluators.
  • Issues found are limited by the evaluator's skill level.
  • Testing carries many assumptions about which usability standards apply.
