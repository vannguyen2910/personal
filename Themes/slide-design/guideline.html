01 · Principles

How the system feels

Four commitments that keep every slide recognisably Winnie's — approachable, structured, and pedagogical.

01

Warm, not corporate

Cream paper, sienna accent, deep-ink text. Avoid startup blue, neon gradients, and sterile white.

02

Bilingual-ready

Type scale leaves room for longer Vietnamese phrases. No cramped columns, no tight leading.

03

Teacher's voice

Numbered steps (01 / 02 / 03), kickers (Phần 1, Milestone 2), and "Ví dụ" callouts mirror Winnie's structure.

04

One idea per slide

Every layout earns its place with a kicker, a title, and a focused body. Frameworks are first-class, not bolted on.

03 · Type

Fraunces × DM Sans

A warm editorial serif for voice and numerals, paired with a clean humanist sans for body — both support Vietnamese diacritics beautifully.

Display · Fraunces
Day 01

Used for slide titles, big numerals, and quoted statements. Italic is the voice of the system — we reach for it on highlights and accents, never as body.

Body · DM Sans
Làm rõ vấn đề
trước khi giải quyết.

Used for body, bullets, and captions. Humanist geometry keeps long Vietnamese phrases readable; tabular numerals for data.
